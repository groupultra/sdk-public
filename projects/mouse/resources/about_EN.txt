---------------------------------------------------------
🧩 How to play 🧩
(1) Click "Select Riddle", which is above your message area ↙
(2) Choose one from the drop down menu
(3) Write your guess to the answer in a message
(3) Send your guess to LLM (Find LLM: Click "All" that's next to your "Send" button; switch it to "∞", yes LLM is infinity and everything)
(4) I'll tell you if you hacked it!

The more LLM Riddle you solve, the higher you are in our Hall of Fame! Click the "Stage" button on the top-right ↗ to see Hall of Fame
